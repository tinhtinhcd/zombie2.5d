# STEP 07 — Polish and Game Feel

## 🎯 Goal

Improve the overall **feel and responsiveness** of the game without adding major new systems.

This step focuses on:

* feedback
* responsiveness
* visual clarity
* player satisfaction

---

# 🧠 Core Principle

> A simple game with good feel is better than a complex game with bad feel.

---

# 🧩 Scope of This Step

You must improve:

* shooting feel
* enemy hit feedback
* player feedback
* UI responsiveness
* visual clarity

You must NOT:

* add new systems (boss, pets, inventory, etc.)
* redesign gameplay structure
* overcomplicate visuals

---

# 🔫 Shooting Feel

## Improvements

* small delay tuning (fire rate feels responsive)
* projectile speed tuning
* visible projectile direction

---

## Feedback

* muzzle flash (simple effect or placeholder)
* slight visual indication when shooting

---

# 💥 Hit Feedback

## On Enemy Hit

* small flash or color change
* slight scale change (optional)
* quick reaction effect

---

## On Enemy Death

* simple effect:

  * fade out
  * quick shrink
  * basic particle (optional)

---

# 🧍 Player Feedback

## On Damage

* screen flash (red tint)
* small camera shake (very subtle)
* HP UI reacts

---

## On Level Up

* clear visual moment:

  * pause
  * glow
  * UI emphasis

---

# 📈 XP Feedback

* XP pickup should:

  * be visible
  * move slightly toward player (optional)
* XP bar:

  * animate when filling

---

# 🎮 UI Feedback

## Buttons

* hover/focus state
* pressed state
* disabled state clearly visible

---

## Transitions

* simple fade in/out between screens
* no instant harsh switching

---

# 🎥 Camera Feel

## Optional improvements

* very subtle camera shake on:

  * shooting
  * taking damage

---

## Rules

* keep it minimal
* avoid motion sickness

---

# 🧠 Gameplay Feel Tuning

Adjust:

* player speed
* enemy speed
* spawn rate
* fire rate

---

## Goal

* early game feels manageable
* later feels intense
* player feels progression

---

# ⚖️ Balance Basics

## Rules

* player must feel stronger over time
* enemies must scale gradually
* avoid sudden difficulty spikes

---

# 🎨 Visual Clarity

* player must be clearly visible
* enemies must be distinguishable
* projectiles must be readable

---

# 📱 Mobile Consideration

* avoid too many particles
* keep effects lightweight
* maintain performance

---

# 🤖 Codex Instructions

* Work with existing systems
* Only add lightweight improvements
* Avoid building complex VFX systems
* Keep effects simple and clear
* Focus on responsiveness

---

# 📦 Deliverables

Provide:

1. improved shooting feedback
2. enemy hit feedback
3. player damage feedback
4. UI responsiveness improvements
5. tuning values used
6. summary of feel improvements

---

# 🎯 Acceptance Criteria

This step is complete when:

* shooting feels responsive
* hits feel impactful
* player understands what is happening
* UI feels interactive
* game feels more polished than raw MVP

---

# 🧠 Final Principle

> Feel turns a prototype into a game.
